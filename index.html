Introduction:

This Vpm Cricket Academy is a dedicated institution where aspiring cricketers, from beginners to advanced players, come to develop their cricketing skills and knowledge. It serves as a nurturing ground for the next generation of cricketing talent, providing them with the essential training, coaching, and exposure needed to excel in the sport of cricket.

Our Tournaments:

  • T20 Format
    • A T20 match is limited to a maximum of 20 overs per side, with each over consisting of six legal deliveries (bowled by the bowler). This results in a concise and action-packed game that typically lasts around three hours.
  • 100 Balls Format
    • In the 100-ball format, each team faces 100 deliveries (balls) during their innings. This contrasts with the traditional Twenty20 (T20) format, which consists of 120 deliveries (20 overs of 6 balls each).
  • Local Tournaments
    • A local tournament, often referred to as a "community tournament" or "local competition," is a sporting event organized at the grassroots level within a specific geographic area or community. These tournaments are typically smaller in scale compared to national or international competitions but play a vital role in promoting sports, fostering community spirit, and providing a platform for local talent to showcase their skills.

Fees Structure:

  • Registration Fee:
    • Many academies require a one-time registration fee when you first enroll. This fee typically covers administrative costs and may range from a nominal amount to a substantial fee.
  • Equipment and Kit Costs:
    • You'll need cricket gear, including bats, balls, protective equipment, and uniforms. Some academies include this in their fees, while others may charge separately or expect you to purchase your gear.
  • Tournament and Travel Expenses:
    • If the academy participates in tournaments or matches, there may be additional costs associated with travel, accommodation, and entry fees.
  • Personal Coaching:
    • If you opt for one-on-one coaching or personalized training sessions, expect to pay extra for these services.
  • Transportation:
    • If the academy is far from your home, you might need to consider transportation costs.
  • Additional Services:
    • Some academies offer services like fitness training, mental conditioning, or nutritional guidance. These services may come with their own fees.
  • Membership Fees:
    • Some academies offer membership options that provide additional benefits, such as priority access to coaching slots or discounts on other services.
  • Refund Policy:
    • Understand the academy's refund policy in case you need to withdraw for any reason.
